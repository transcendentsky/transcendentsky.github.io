## Profile

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Application Technology from the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. My long-term research and application work focuses on the iteration and optimization of reliable intelligent systems. Starting from few-shot and one-shot feature matching, active sample selection, and data augmentation, my work has gradually expanded to multimodal medical diagnosis, interactive segmentation, and industry-oriented diagnostic agents.

My research emphasizes the continuous optimization capability of models in real-world scenarios. Through data selection, feature alignment, interactive feedback, model evaluation, and business-loop integration, I aim to build AI systems that not only work under few-shot settings, but also continuously improve stability and reliability under multi-sample and complex industry data.
